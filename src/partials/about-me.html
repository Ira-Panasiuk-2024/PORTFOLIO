About me

My story

I'm Ira, and my journey into the world of web development began with a simple curiosity: how are the websites we use every day created? This curiosity grew into a real passion. After completing intensive courses in full-stack development, I realized that this is exactly what I want to do. I am fascinated by the whole process: from the first idea to the finished product that users can see and feel.

What inspires me?

The opportunity to combine creativity with technology. Every project is a new challenge, a new opportunity to learn something interesting and create something useful. As a full-stack developer, I understand the importance of every detail - from page loading speed to intuitive interface. I strive to create not just websites, but digital experiences that are memorable.

Always open to new projects and opportunities for professional growth!
